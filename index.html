About me

My name is Liam Pretorius.
I tragically died at home falling out of a tree.
I was a passionate, loving and caring boy. I adored the outdoors and you would regularly find me busy In my forest playing and dreaming. I loved animals and birds. I enjoyed making people laugh and I gave the best hugs. I really enjoyed dressing nicely, but I also loved to go camping with my co-scouts. There we will never shower, just swim. I left behind my big brother and Mommy and Daddy.
